Galaxy II Secure DCT HMAC-based Scrambler for WAV files


Requirements:

Python 3.7+ (preferably 3.8 or newer)
numpy
soundfile
scipy
argparse

To Scramble
galaxy2.py --action scramble --input clear.wav --output scrambled.wav --key 1122334455667788aabbccddeeff1122

To Descramble
galaxy2.py --action descramble --input scrambled.wav --output recovered.wav --key 1122334455667788aabbccddeeff1122 

Options:

--segment_ms   default: 40
--key          Up to 128-bit strength (8-32 hexadecimal characters)
--no-post_lpf  Skip 4 kHz low-pass after descramble
